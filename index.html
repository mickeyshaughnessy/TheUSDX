US Federal Data Exchange

A privacy-preserving interface for individual-level government records. Submit natural-language queries — our AI matches the right dataset and automatically redacts names, addresses, dates, and other PII before returning results.

How it works: Describe the records you need (personnel files, contractor histories, municipal service records, etc.). The AI selects the matching dataset and replaces identifying details with realistic equivalents — "Maria Vasquez" becomes "Jennifer Park", "8412 W Colfax Ave" becomes "3201 S Wadsworth Blvd" — preserving record structure while protecting individual privacy.

Features

AI Matching

Describe records in plain English — AI finds the right dataset.

Smart Redaction

PII is replaced with realistic equivalents, not blacked out.

Cloud Infrastructure

Digital Ocean Spaces with S3-compatible object storage.

Secure Access

JWT authentication and HTTPS support.
